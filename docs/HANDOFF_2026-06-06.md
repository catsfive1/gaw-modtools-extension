## 7. ELI5 — how the AI username bot detector works

A **3-stage bouncer + a raid radar**:

1. **Free pattern check (T0, instant, no AI).** Score each new username on shape: trailing run of 3+ digits (+20); dictionary words glued with a 3-digit tail like `blackleopard480` (+20); low letter-entropy (+15); matches a known bot shape-family (+30). Bands: **≤29 SKIP** (wave through) · **30–59 ADJUDICATE** (ask the AI) · **≥60 ESCALATE** (straight to SUS). Free + instant → AI budget only spent on the ambiguous middle.
2. **AI judgment (T1, Llama, only the 30–59 band).** Fuzzy bot-or-not on the borderline names — catches mutations the rules miss (leet, separators, real-name camouflage). Risk ≥60 → SUS.
3. **Human (always).** Flagged → **SUS list, NEVER auto-banned (HI-1)**. Mod removes false positives, one-click **flushes** real bots to Death Row (72h delay).

**Raid radar:** SUS users grouped by `braidFamilyClass` (`blackleopard480`→`A.Db`). **≥5 same-family in 15 min** (or 15 in 2h) → one deduped alert (60-min cooldown) to mod-chat + Discord.
**Self-improving (designed, not built):** flush = confirmed-bot (high weight), remove = false-positive (low weight) → few-shot feedback to sharpen the scorer as the enemy mutates.

Worker fns: `t0Prefilter` · `braidScoreViaLlama` · `braidPlaceSus` (score) · `braidFamilyClass`/`braidDetectAlarms`/`braidIncidentDedup` (raid) · `braidDispatchRaidAlert` (notify). Routes `/raid/score-candidates`, `/raid/detect`. **All DEPLOYED.**
